About Us

Sajjalashree Home Nursing is a leading home nursing service provider in Bangalore. We provide home nursing services for elderly people, patients, and new mothers. Our services include nursing care, physiotherapy, and medical equipment rental. We have a team of experienced nurses and physiotherapists who provide quality care to our patients. Our goal is to provide compassionate care to our patients and help them recover quickly. We are committed to providing the best home nursing services in Bangalore.

Services

We provide a wide range of home nursing services to cater to the needs of our patients. Our services include:

ELDER CARE

At SAJJALASHREE HOME NURSING SERVICES, we provide personalized elder care services to ensure comfort, dignity, and well-being for your loved ones. Our professional caregivers and nurses are dedicated to offering compassionate support right in the comfort of home.

  • Personalized Nursing Care
  • Daily Living Assistance
  • Nutritional Support & Meal Preparation
  • Physical Therapy & Rehabilitation
  • 24/7 Nursing Care (If Needed)

POST OPERATIVE CARE

Recovering from surgery requires specialized care and attention to ensure a safe, smooth, and speedy recovery. At SAJJALASHREE HOME NURSING SERVICES, we provide professional post-operative care services to help patients heal comfortably at home with the right medical support and personal assistance.

  • Wound Care & Dressing Changes
  • Medication Management
  • Pain Management & Monitoring
  • 24/7 Nursing Care (If Needed)

HOSPITAL SPECIAL NURSING CARE

At SAJJALASHREE HOME NURSING SERVICES, we offer specialized nursing care for patients who require expert medical attention during their hospital stay. Our highly trained nurses provide personalized, round-the-clock care, ensuring comfort, safety, and a faster recovery.

  • Post-Surgical Nursing Support
  • Pain Management & Vital Signs Monitoring
  • Medication Administration
  • Certified & Experienced Nurses

MOTHER AND BABY CARE

Welcoming a baby is a beautiful journey, but it also comes with the need for specialized care for both the mother and newborn. At SAJJALASHREE HOME NURSING SERVICES, we provide professional mother and baby care services to ensure a safe, comfortable, and healthy postnatal experience.

  • Post-Delivery Recovery Support
  • Emotional & Psychological Support
  • Gentle Baby Bathing & Hygiene
  • Sleep & Feeding Guidance

BEDSIDE ATTENDANT SERVICES AT HOME

Caring for a loved one who is bedridden or requires continuous assistance can be challenging. At SAJJALASHREE HOME NURSING SERVICES, we offer professional bedside attendant services to ensure comfort, dignity, and proper support for patients in the comfort of their homes.

  • Personal Hygiene Assistance
  • Medication Reminders
  • Companionship & Emotional Support
  • Light Housekeeping & Assistance

PERSONAL SUPPORTIVE CARE

At SAJJALASHREE HOME NURSING SERVICES, we understand that some individuals need extra support in their daily lives due to age, illness, disability, or post-surgical recovery. Our Personal Supportive Care Services provide compassionate, hands-on assistance to ensure comfort, independence, and well-being in the familiar surroundings of home.

  • Meal Preparation & Feeding Assistance
  • Daily Living Assistance
  • Light Housekeeping
  • Errands & Escort Services

DEMENTIA CARE

Caring for a loved one with dementia or Alzheimer’s requires patience, understanding, and professional expertise. At SAJJALASHREE HOME NURSING SERVICES, we provide specialized dementia care services to ensure safety, comfort, and emotional well-being for individuals dealing with memory loss and cognitive decline.

  • Personalized Daily Care
  • Daily Living Assistance
  • Memory Care Activities
  • Companionship & Social Engagement

AMBULANCE SERVICES 24 HOURS

Medical emergencies require quick action and professional care. At SAJJALASHREE HOME NURSING SERVICES, we provide 24-hour ambulance services to ensure safe, fast, and efficient transportation for patients in need of urgent or non-emergency medical care.

  • Non-Emergency Medical Transport
  • Basic & Advanced Life Support Ambulance
  • Inter-Hospital Transfers
  • Event Medical Coverage
